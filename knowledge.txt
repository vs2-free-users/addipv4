##English

What to do when something goes wrong?

- Login into the customer panel on https://support.euserv.com
- Click on vserver tab
- Select your vps contract
- Click on Rescue System
- Click on Activate
- Wait until you receive email that rescue is ready
- Go to your server via ssh
- Got to path
/mnt/customer
- Here are your normal os, now you can restore your config backup
- Disable the rescue system on the same way
